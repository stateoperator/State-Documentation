## What is STATE?

STATE is a prediction market platform built on Solana. Every market on STATE is backed by a real-world news event — discovered automatically from global news sources and turned into a tradeable token.

Unlike traditional prediction markets, every bet you place is a token purchase on a bonding curve. This means markets have real price action, constant buy pressure, and winners receive token payouts they can sell.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Events are discovered">
    STATE's AI pipeline (ARK2) monitors 20+ global news sources around the clock. When a significant, binary-outcome event emerges — a rate decision, election result, geopolitical move — ARK2 generates a market for it automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="A token is launched">
    Each event becomes a bonding curve token on Solana (e.g. `$FEDRATE`, `$BTCQ1`). The price rises with every buy — no liquidity pools, no order books.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You take a position">
    Bet YES or NO on the outcome. Your bet buys tokens on the bonding curve and locks them in escrow. Normal trading continues alongside the prediction market.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Event resolves">
    When the event resolves, winners receive the accumulated token pool proportional to their stake. Losers receive nothing. Winners can sell their tokens on the bonding curve for SOL.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why STATE?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Every bet creates buy pressure">
    On STATE, placing a bet and buying a token are the same action. This means prediction markets and token speculation reinforce each other — more bettors = higher token price = more speculation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Real events, not synthetic ones">
    Markets are sourced from real news — Reuters, AP, Bloomberg, and 17 other feeds. STATE doesn't create hypothetical events; it turns the news into markets.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Winners get tokens, not just SOL">
    When you win, you receive the accumulated token pool. You decide when to sell. If the market narrative continues to grow after resolution, your payout grows too.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
